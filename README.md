# EV Market & Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

The Electric Vehicle (EV) industry is growing rapidly due to advancements in battery technology, increasing environmental awareness, and evolving consumer preferences.

This project analyzes EV market and sales data to uncover key factors influencing vehicle pricing, customer satisfaction, battery performance, and market trends.

The analysis combines Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), data visualization, and Machine Learning techniques to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze EV market trends and pricing patterns
- Explore relationships between battery capacity and driving range
- Identify factors influencing customer satisfaction
- Detect and understand outliers in EV specifications
- Build predictive models for sales/performance analysis
- Generate business recommendations based on data-driven insights

## Key Insights

### EV Market Trends
- EV prices vary significantly across market segments.
- Premium vehicles generally command higher prices.

### Battery and Range
- Battery capacity shows a strong positive relationship with driving range.
- Larger batteries typically support longer travel distances.

### Customer Satisfaction
- Driving range is one of the strongest factors influencing customer ratings.
- Charging performance and warranty support also contribute positively.

### Market Positioning
- Brand value and perceived reliability influence customer purchasing decisions.
- Performance and pricing alone do not guarantee market success.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus on improving driving range to increase customer satisfaction.
- Optimize battery efficiency to improve value proposition.
- Strengthen brand trust through warranty and after-sales support.
- Balance performance and affordability for broader market adoption.
